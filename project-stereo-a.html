Stereo A: Video Player

Role

Lead UI Designer

Timeline

3 Months (2023)

Tools

Figma, Principle, After Effects

The Challenge

Most modern video players are cluttered with controls that distract from the cinematic experience. The challenge was to create a gesture-driven interface that remains completely invisible during playback, while providing powerful tools for professional editors and casual viewers alike.

visibility_off
Zero Friction

Reducing UI presence during active viewing to prioritize content immersion.

touch_app
Gesture Logic

Mapping complex controls (scrubbing, brightness, volume) to intuitive touch gestures.

The Solution

A Narrative-First Interface

Stereo A utilizes a progressive disclosure model. Controls are contextually aware, appearing only when the user's focus shifts. We developed a proprietary "Fluid Scrub" system that allows for frame-perfect precision without a physical seek bar.

  • check Dynamic contrast adjustment based on video color palette
  • check Minimalist logo and branding integration
Results & Impact

40% Increase in session duration through focused viewing.

By removing the "clutter" of traditional players, users reported a much higher level of satisfaction and emotional connection to the media they were consuming.
